## Why Thirdweb?

Using Thirdweb is recommended because it eliminates the complexity of Web3 development, focusing on building rather than managing blockchain intricacies.

- Pre-built Smart Contracts
- SDKs & APIs
- Multi-chain Support
- Wallet & Payments
- Dashboard Management
